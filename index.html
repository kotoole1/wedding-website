Our story

Kevin and Kelsey met through their love of music and laughter.

We both attended the Olin College of Engineering in Needham, Massachusetts, however, were in different graduating classes (Kelsey 2014 and Kevin 2015) and somehow managed to just miss meeting each other in a school of ~300 people! After graduating college, we both independently chose to stay and work in the Boston area, and eventually ended up meeting, thanks to many college friends we had in common.

It turns out we had many interests in common as well, and we grew to be good friends through singing in the “Infrequencies” acapella group, playing in the local HONK marching band (Kevin on saxophone and Kelsey on piccolo), and performing piano and vocal duets together.

Now, our happiness together is magnified by our two cats, Legato and Glissando, and we are very glad to celebrate and share our love for each other with you this June 10th, 2023 at our wedding ceremony!
