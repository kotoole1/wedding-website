Our story


Kevin O'Toole and Kelsey Schilperoort met through their love of music and laughter.

We both attended the Olin College of Engineering in Needham, but somehow managed to miss meeting each other in a school of ~300 people! After graduating, we both stayed and work in the Boston area, and eventually ended up meeting, thanks to many college friends we had in common. It turns out we had many interests in common as well, and we grew to be good friends through singing in the “Infrequencies” acapella group, playing in the local HONK marching band (Kevin on saxophone and Kelsey on piccolo), playing piano and vocal duets, solving puzzles and playing games together.

Kevin proposed in Boulder, Colorado with a puzzle hidden at the end of an escape room we solved together. During our long engagement, we have added two wonderful cats to our home, Legato and Glissando, and discovered a life we love living together.

We are so excited to finally celebrate it with you in June!

Dress

Garden Semi-Formal


We are celebrating, so let's not let the flowers be the only bright colors!

Weather in June is expected to be mildly warm (average high of 75). If you choose to wear a suit or sport coat, rest assured your seat at the reception table will be available all wedding long to leave your jacket whenever you'd like.

During the short ceremony, guests will mainly be standing (chairs will be provided for guests requesting accommodation). Please wear footwear appropriate for walking on grassy/uneven surfaces and which you would be comfortable standing in for at least 15-20 minutes.
